# Objection: Price Concern

## Common user concern

The customer says the solution is too expensive.

## Response strategy

Acknowledge the concern, then discuss total value, operational efficiency, implementation support, and long-term fit.

## Safe response

I understand budget is important. Instead of looking only at upfront price, we can also compare the total value, the work it may save, and the support available during rollout.

## Caution

Do not claim exact savings, return on investment, or competitive superiority unless those claims are directly supported by approved source material.
